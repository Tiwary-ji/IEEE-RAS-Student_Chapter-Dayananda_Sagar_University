Rapid Tracer

Date: May 3rd, 2024

Time: 09:30 AM - 4:00 PM

Location: Lecture Hall 2

"Rapid Tracer", held on May 3, 2024, was a thrilling robotics competition organized by the Department of Computer Science & Engineering (AI & ML) at Dayananda Sagar University, in collaboration with the Yantrove Robotics Club and IEEE RAS SBC Society. The event showcased innovative line-following robots, engineered to navigate challenging courses with speed and precision. Over three competitive rounds, participants demonstrated technical expertise, fostering a spirit of innovation and collaboration. With esteemed guests and faculty present, the event was a testament to the potential of robotics and a platform for students to showcase their creativity and problem-solving skills.
